Notice to Residents

Dear Residents of First Ballston Commons

Our community is home to a wide range of households, including young families, young adults, and empty-nesters. We also have a mix of owner-occupied and renter-occupied homes.

We seek to maintain a beautiful, friendly community, and we ask that everyone do their part.

Parking reminders

Assigned parking

Park where assigned — every home has one assigned parking spot. If you (or your guest) park in another spot, you may be towed.

Street parking

Limited street parking is available. You can apply for a Residential Parking Permit from the county: arlingtonva.us/Parking/Residential-Permit-Parking.

Noise reminders

Noise sometimes becomes a problem, especially in good weather.

  • check_circle Our shared walls transfer sound; please keep music and TV volumes reasonable.
  • check_circle The configuration of our units acts like a speaker box and amplifies even the mildest conversation so that it carries easily to your neighbors.
  • warning It is a violation of Arlington County law to make loud noises outdoors after 9:00 p.m.
  • attach_money Recently the county fine for excessive noise went up from $25 to “not less than $100.”

Trash can etiquette

Trash day is Tuesday

If Tuesday is a holiday, trash is collected on Wednesday.

Put out after

5pm Monday

Return before

8am Wednesday

Containers: Trash should only be put out in containers provided by Arlington County (Trash Carts – black, Recycling Carts – blue, Yard Waste Carts – brown).

Labeling: Trash containers should be labeled with your street address. Unlabeled containers may be returned to the county by the Homeowner’s Association.

Timing: Put containers out after 5pm Monday and return them before 8am Wednesday.

Damaged containers: If your container is damaged, contact Arlington County at 703-228-5000 for a replacement.

Excess trash: May be put out in sturdy plastic bags (please avoid when possible, as it encourages rodents!).

Prevent pests: Keep trash secure with lids closed in containers assigned by Arlington County, and please do not move rat traps.

For more information please see the Arlington VA website at: https://recycling.arlingtonva.us/residential/trash-recycling/trash-recycling-carts/

The final word on rules is in our condo documents, available in the resident portal.
