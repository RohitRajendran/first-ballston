Welcome to First Ballston Commons

First Ballston Commons is one of the oldest townhouse developments in Ballston. Our community is a mix of homeowners and renters. We have reserved parking for each unit, trash removal on Tuesdays, and grounds maintenance provided by Blade Runners. This website provides information for residents and homeowners. Please contact the First Ballston Commons Board if you have additional questions.

For residents

Trash & recycling

Pickup on Tuesdays, set out after 5pm Monday, and store by 8am Wednesday.

Parking

One reserved spot per home; parking in another spot may result in towing.
